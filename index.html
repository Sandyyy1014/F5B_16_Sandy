About Me

My name is Sandy. I was born on October 14, 2007, and I am a recent graduate of St. Joseph's Parish Secondary School No. 5 in Macau.


I took Economics, Accounting, and Advanced Math in my freshman year of high school. My future college majors are Civil Engineering and Architecture. My hobbies are music, photography and art. I have participated in the school choir and photography class. I think choir can help me understand the importance of unity; photography can help me record the angles and shapes of different things, and record special architectural styles and features, so that I can appreciate and study them over and over again; and art can train me to have control over lines, which is very important when drawing graphic designs or tracing buildings by hand.


I may be an introvert and a slow learner, but I think extroverts have an advantage, so I participate in a lot of extracurricular activities and part-time jobs to make more friends and train my extroverted personality. Sometimes I also work as a volunteer to help people with special needs or mental retardation, caring for them.
